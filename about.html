ABOUT US

Our Story

The Spark of Brotherhood

Golden Spoon Restaurant began as a cozy family dream, brought to life by two adventurous brothers in the vibrant heart of Harare. Bound by a shared passion for outdoor cooking and a promise to use only the freshest local ingredients, we set out to create more than just a place to eat. We wanted to build a dining experience that bursts with the bold, unforgettable flavors of expertly grilled and smoked meats.

Flavors Built on Passion

Every dish we serve carries the essence of that original spark. From the perfect sear on our grills to the deep, rich notes of our slow-smoked specialties, we pour our hearts into every recipe. We believe that good food has the power to bring people together, and we strive to make every meal a celebration of flavor and togetherness.
